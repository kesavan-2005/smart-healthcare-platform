PROBLEM STATEMENT 
Lack of transparency reduces trust in AI systems.
Systems are not user-friendly for elderly and disabled users.
No proper emergency detection for critical conditions.
Patients face high costs due to unnecessary tests.
Absence of integrated healthcare solutions in a single platform.


--- SLIDE 3 ---
INTRODUCTION
The project presents an AI-driven smart healthcare platform that functions as a digital hospital.
It enables multimodal interaction (voice and text input) for better accessibility.
The system provides instant disease prediction with emergency alerts for critical cases.
It is designed as a scalable solution for future integration with hospitals and telemedicine services.


--- SLIDE 4 ---
ABSTRACT
The completed modules include voice-based input, an AI-driven disease prediction system, and SHAP-based visualization for explainable results, all integrated with a fully functional frontend, backend, and cloud deployment. The remaining work focuses on implementing OCR for medical report scanning and a doctor recommendation system to enhance user support. In the future, the system will include video consultation and digital health records, with a modular design that allows easy addition of new features and scalability.

--- SLIDE 8 ---
TOOLS AND TECHNOLOGIES
Frontend: Tailwind CSS, JavaScript, React.js Web Speech API.
Backend: Python and Flask framework.
AI Tools: Scikit-learn, Pandas, SHAP.
Deployment: Render, Gunicorn, GitHub.
Development supported by VS Code and virtual environments.
